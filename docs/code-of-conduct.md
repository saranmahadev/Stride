# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We pledge to make participation in Stride a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age, body size, disability, ethnicity, gender identity and expression, level of experience, education, socio-economic status, nationality, personal appearance, race, religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

---

## Our Standards

### ✅ Positive Behavior

- Using welcoming and inclusive language
- Being respectful of differing viewpoints
- Gracefully accepting constructive criticism
- Focusing on what is best for the community
- Showing empathy towards others

### ❌ Unacceptable Behavior

- Trolling, insulting, or derogatory comments
- Public or private harassment
- Publishing others' private information
- Other conduct inappropriate in a professional setting
